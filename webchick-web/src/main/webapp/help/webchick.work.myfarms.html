My farms

After login to the system user can access to own farm list . In this case the cellink (alarm system) representing the farm . Each cellink has a name, state and version . If cellink is online then button "Show houses" will appear near the icon with hen and chicken . It mean that you can press on icon or press on "Show houses" button and start loading controller data . Otherwise the icon with hen and chicken will clouded and button "Show houses" disappear .
